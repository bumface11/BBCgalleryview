# Privacy Policy for BBC News Gallery Viewer

## No Data Collection
This extension does not collect, store, or share any personal data. It only processes webpage content on BBC News ("Your Pictures" articles) to extract images for the gallery.

## Permissions & Justification
The extension requests limited permissions:
- `activeTab` & `scripting`: To extract images from the active page.
- `host_permissions` for `bbc.co.uk`: To ensure images are correctly processed.
